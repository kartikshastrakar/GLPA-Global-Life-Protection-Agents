# Data Sources Registry

This document tracks all data inputs used by the Global Life Protection Agents (GLPA) system. All sources must meet the criteria for public availability and non-personal data.

## Data Governance Criteria
To be included in this system, a data source must:
1. Be accessible to the public or via official NGO/Governmental API.
2. Contain zero Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
3. Represent population-level trends or macro-economic/social indicators.

---

## Approved Data Sources

| Source Name | Category | Public Availability | Non-Personal Confirmation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **UN OCHA (ReliefWeb) Reports** | NGO / Humanitarian | Yes (Public API/Web) | Confirmed: Aggregated field reports only. |
| **ACLED (Conflict Data)** | Conflict Trends | Yes (Public Export) | Confirmed: Event-based, no individual tracking. |
| **WHO Mental Health Atlas** | Health Statistics | Yes (Global Reports) | Confirmed: National/Regional statistics only. |
| **World Bank Open Data** | Socio-Economic | Yes (Public API) | Confirmed: Macro-economic indicators. |

---

## Prohibited Data Categories
The following data types are strictly blocked from being integrated into the watsonx Orchestrate workflow:
* **Social Media Scraped Data:** High risk of PII and bias.
* **Private Medical Records:** Violation of HIPAA/GDPR and project scope.
* **Crisis Hotline Transcripts:** Sensitive, individual-level data.
* **Law Enforcement Databases:** Out of scope (Prevention only, not enforcement).
